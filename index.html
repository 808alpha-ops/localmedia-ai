About

Guy Tasaka has spent 40 years in media — from the New York Times to building the first digital subscription products, from launching Barnes & Noble's Nook content pipeline to advising local publishers on paid content strategy.

Now he writes about AI in local media for Editor & Publisher and speaks at industry conferences. This newsletter extends that work with weekly, actionable insights.

Jayd is Guy's AI partner — not a chatbot, but a genuine collaborator in research, writing, and operations. This newsletter is written together, demonstrating the partnership model we advocate.
